Frequently Asked Questions

Home FAQ

Which projects are currently available for booking on the North Coast?

El Amar Group currently offers units in several North Coast developments such as: Shores at km 75, Bella Mera at km 74, in addition to delivered resorts like Krair Lagoon, Marina Flowers and Marina Sun Shine. For up-to-date availability and unit types, you can call our hotline 19783 or visit our sales offices.

What payment plans are available? Do you require down payments and delivery payments?

Payment plans vary by project, but typically range between 3 and 8 years. Usually there is a reservation down payment, followed by quarterly or semi-annual installments, and a delivery payment upon handover. Full payment details and schedules are clarified in the sales contract for each project.

Are El Amar projects fully licensed and legally registered?

Yes. All El Amar projects are developed under the required licenses issued by the relevant authorities. Units are registered according to the procedures applicable in each area. Legal integrity and documentation are core pillars of our long-term relationship with our clients since 1986.

Are the units delivered fully finished or semi-finished?

The finishing level depends on the project. Many coastal resorts are delivered fully finished (including kitchens and bathrooms), while some residential towers in Cairo or Alexandria may be delivered semi-finished according to the product positioning. The finishing specification is clearly defined in each contract.

Are there maintenance or service charges? How are they calculated?

Yes. In gated coastal resorts and residential compounds, there is an annual maintenance/service fee that covers common-area services such as security, cleaning, landscaping, pool operations and facility upkeep. The fee is usually calculated based on the unit’s area and the project’s service level, and is specified in the sales and maintenance agreements.

How can I reserve a unit or schedule a site visit?

You can reserve a unit or arrange a site visit through: visiting our sales offices in Heliopolis or Sporting (Alexandria), calling the hotline 19783, or sending us a request through the Contact Us page. Our sales team will coordinate a suitable time and explain all details.

Do you offer after-sales services such as resale or rental management?

Yes. For selected projects, El Amar Group (or its operating partners) offers after-sales support, including assistance with resale within the project or seasonal rental management in certain coastal resorts. The availability and fees of these services differ by project and are clarified upon request.

How can I stay updated with new launches and special offers?

You can stay updated through: our official website, social media pages (Facebook and Instagram), or by contacting the hotline 19783 and requesting to be added to our client database, so that you receive invitations and announcements for new launches and offers.

El Amar Group is a leading player in Egypt’s coastal and residential real estate market. Since 1986, we have developed 14 coastal resorts and 56 residential towers, delivering over 11,000 units nationwide.
