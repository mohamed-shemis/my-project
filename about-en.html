Leading Coastal & Residential Developer Since 1986

El Amar Group is one of the early pioneers in Egypt’s real estate development sector. Since 1986, the Group has successfully delivered a diverse portfolio of residential, commercial and coastal projects across the country—built in strategic locations and delivered on or ahead of schedule.
